The Dashboard makes it easy to get to campaigns, surveys, and your response history

Campaigns

On the Campaign List screen you can see the campaigns you are participating in (which have already been downloaded to the phone) as well as campaigns available for participation (which are on the server, available to be downloaded to the phone). You can access information about the campaigns you are participating in, as well as set up triggered reminders.

Surveys

On the Survey List screen you can see and take surveys from all the campaigns you are participating in.

Response History

On the Response History screen you can see calendar and map views of your response history.

Upload Queue

In the Upload Queue you can manage the upload status of the responses you have just completed.

Profile

On the Profile screen you can update your password, or logout and wipe your data.
